Sponsors

We would like to thank our very generous sponsors, without whom we would never have been able to find the amazing success that we have had. Below we have listed everyone who has contributed to our success in rank order of how important they were. While of course there were many others that have contributed to our success we cannot list everyone here, however, we have put the most recent impacts on our band. We hope that no one is offended or feels hurt just because they didn't make the list, know that we are thankful for everyone, but cannot spend days putting them all onto this page.

We would also like to dedicate a special paragraph to Darn Tough, which is our first sponser below. They inspired much of our artwork, more than that, they provide us with fresh clean socks for every single gig we get, which means that we get about 200 free pairs of socks every year. We really <3 them.

Another special sponsor we must mention is Bombas, who supply some of the socks we wear on stage! For every pair of socks we sell Bombas delivers one more pair of socks to those who truly need it. We are absolutely stoked that they have decided to sponsor Honolulu's Angriest Argyle in our ongoing quest to fight terrible socks and at the same time blast them off you when we rock out! Again we are super stoked to do business with them.
